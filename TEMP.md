Automatically Included:

Add-ons (per night):
premium_day (+20%)
second_dog (+15)
additional_dog (+10) for third, fourth, etc.
additional_cat (+8)
additional_other (+5) (types listed below, fish are free)  
additional_mile (+2.5)  
additional_exercise (+5) for each added 15 min of exercise
needy_A (+5) if any pet cannot be left alone for at least 6 hrs
needy_B (+5) if any pet cannot be left alone for at least 4 hours
needy_C (+5) if any pet cannot be left alone for at least 2 hours
needy_D (+5) if any pet cannot be left alone at all

Auto-Discounts (per night):
cat_only (-25) or for no-animal/exotic animal households
puppy_household (-10) for any household with one or more <6 mo dog/s
long_A (-5%) for stays over 4 days
long_B (-5%) for stays over 6 days

Discounts (per night, manually input/never auto-offered to client):
kiche_discount (-15%)

\*No constraints listed bc I need to confirm all house-sits manually (no auto-booking). If over 15mi away though it can let them know I’m unlikely to accept the booking.
