The mark sits in the header beside Fraunces headings and Public Sans body, on the warm Trail palette. Three directions below — each a real take on the name, not a font sampler. Toggle dark to judge contrast both ways.

My pick — a signed name

Cal Barba

Dog Walking & House Sitting

Caveat, the felt-tip signature. A solo keeper-of-dogs is trusted as a person, not a logo — a handwritten name reads like Cal signed the card themselves. It's warm and human without tipping into wedding-invite territory, stays legible at nav size, and leaves all the editorial weight to Fraunces so the two never compete. The clipped Public Sans caps underneath do the literal job — saying what the business is — so the script is free to be personality.
